Photographs

I have loved taking pictures all my life. I bought myself my first digital camera when I was 8 years old afer saving up all of my Christmas and birthday money. Since then, I have upgraded my technology quite a bit to a DSLR camera. I am still learning how to use it, and therefore my photos aren't the greatest, but I enjoy getting outside and practicing and continually learning.
